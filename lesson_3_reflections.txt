When would you want to use a remote repository rather than keeping all your work local?
When you feel your work is ready to be shared publicly. When you are working collaboratively and want other people to have access to your code in order to adapt it, review it or add to it. 

Why might you want to always pull changes manually rather than having Git
automatically stay up-to-date with your remote repository?

You might only want to pull changes when you or the people you are working with are ready to share their code.
